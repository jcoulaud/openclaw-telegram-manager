# Code of Conduct

Based on the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## The short version

Be kind, be respectful, assume good intent. We want everyone to feel welcome here regardless of background or experience level.

## What we expect

- Be welcoming and inclusive
- Respect different viewpoints
- Take constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the project

## What's not okay

- Insults, trolling, or personal attacks
- Harassment of any kind (public or private)
- Sharing someone's private information without permission
- Anything you wouldn't do in a professional setting

## Enforcement

If something's wrong, [open a private report on GitHub](https://github.com/jcoulaud/openclaw-telegram-manager/security/advisories/new) or create an issue. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled fairly.
